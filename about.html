N°001 — About

I'm a neuroscience researcher who codes. At the Gao Research Group at UIC, I develop Expansion Microscopy protocols for nanoscale imaging of mouse brain tissue and build automated analysis pipelines in Python to quantify RNA puncta and characterize neuroinflammatory gene expression in Alzheimer's disease models at single-cell resolution.

At UChicago Medicine, I conduct structured interviews on end-of-life care decision-making and apply R and statistical modeling to identify healthcare utilization disparities in AAPI Medicare beneficiaries — research presented at national conferences.

My work lives at the intersection of wet lab science, computational image analysis, and patient-facing clinical research. Graduating May 2026. Seeking Pre-MD/PhD opportunities.
